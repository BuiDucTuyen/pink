Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We have answers!

  • What is Hyperas Chain?
    The Hyperas Chain is a new and innovative blockchain architecture that utilizes a dual-chain approach. One chain operates on a Proof-of-Work (PoW) AI algorithm, which provides security and decentralization to the network, while the other operates on a Proof-of-Authority (PoA) consensus algorithm, which provides fast and efficient transactions. By utilizing a dual-chain approach, the Hyperas Chain is able to provide the benefits of both PoW and PoA consensus algorithms, resulting in a more secure, scalable, and energy-efficient blockchain architecture.
  • Why is the Hyperas blockchain for everyone?
    Hyperas Chain brings numerous benefits, making it an attractive choice for users and developers alike. It provides a secure environment for people to build blockchain-based solutions and conduct transactions safely and efficiently.
  • What role does HYRA Token play? How is HYRA Token used? What is the total supply of HYRA tokens?
    The HYRA Native Token is the native token of the Hyperas chain, playing a crucial role in fueling the activities and operations within its ecosystem. Designed to be fundamental to the network, the HYRA Native Token acts as the foundation for transactions, governance, and various functionalities across the Hyperas chain. Beyond its primary implementation, HYRA Native Tokens are additionally developed on the Arbitrum network, a Layer 2 scaling solution for Ethereum, to enhance its interoperability and efficiency within the wider blockchain ecosystem. With a total supply of 50 billion HYRA Native Tokens, this currency is aimed at supporting a diverse range of applications, from APP to other blockchain-based applications, enabling smooth transactions and interactions within the Hyperas chain and among interconnected platforms.
  • Does the project have actual products or applications? If so, what are they and what is their development potential?
    Currently, the project has developed an ecosystem consisting of Hyra Scan, Hyra Swap, Hyra Bridge, and Hyra Stake, along with a Dapp called AI Earn Hub. In the future, the project will focus on building a robust and diverse ecosystem. This ecosystem will encompass a range of components and activities to foster a vibrant community and positive development environment. Building a strong and diverse ecosystem will help the project achieve sustainable growth and create long-term value for the community and users.
